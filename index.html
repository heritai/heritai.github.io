About Me

I am a Machine Learning Researcher specializing in AI ethics, multimodal learning, and deep learning systems. With a strong academic background in statistical modeling, reinforcement learning, and symbolic AI, my work bridges the gap between theoretical foundations and real-world applications. Passionate about responsible AI, I have contributed to projects that ensure transparency, fairness, and safety in AI-driven systems.

Research & Publications

My research focuses on ethical AI, computational models for moral reasoning, and multimodal learning. During my PhD at LIP6 (Sorbonne University), I developed logical frameworks to model ethical decision-making in AI systems. I have published research in conferences and workshops related to AI safety, knowledge representation, and machine ethics, contributing to interdisciplinary efforts that combine philosophy, logic, and machine learning.

Projects

I have worked on various AI projects, including:

  • Ethical AI Reasoning Models: Developed logic-based AI systems capable of ethical reasoning.
  • Multimodal Learning: Designed AI models integrating textual and visual information for enhanced decision-making.
  • LLM Safety & Security: Contributed to projects ensuring robustness and fairness in generative AI applications.
  • Symbolic AI & Causality: Researched interpretable AI methods combining logic and machine learning.
